title: Changing user language category: faq section: change_lang

Learn English - Studycat

  1. Select the portal icon.

  1. Select the profile icon.

  1. Select 'Settings'

  1. Complete the age verification by entering the numbers shown on screen in order (for example '912')

  1. Select 'Languages'

  1. Select your preferred language

  1. The app will now display in your selected language

For other Studycat apps

  1. Open your Studycat app and navigate to the 'Parents' area from the main menu

  1. Complete the age verification by entering the numbers shown on screen in order (for example '924')

  1. Select 'Settings'

  1. Select 'Language'

  1. Select your preferred language

  1. The app will now display in your selected language
