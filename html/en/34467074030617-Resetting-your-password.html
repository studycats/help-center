Studycat English and Spanish

  1. Go to the parents section (see Accessing the parents section – Help Centre | Studycat)

  2. Tap the "Sign in/Create an account" button

  1. Select "Sign in with email"

  1. Tap "Forgot your password"

  1. Type your account email address and tap "Send verification code"

  1. Check your email and copy the verification code

  1. Enter the verification code and tap "Verify code". If you need a new code, tap "Send new code"

  1. Create your new password
